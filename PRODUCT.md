# StratOS: Strategic Operating System
## Mission: Architecture for the Power Four Ascent

StratOS is a high-performance **Strategic Operating System** designed for executive leadership teams who need to bridge the gap between high-level vision and tactical execution. Originally developed for the **KSU Athletics "Taking Flight to 2026"** initiative, StratOS transforms static strategic plans into living, agentic ecosystems.

### Core Modules

#### 1. Executive Briefing Console
A premium, high-fidelity dashboard for leaders. It provides a "Command Center" view of strategic pillars, key metrics, and tactical priorities.
- **Dynamic Metrics:** Real-time tracking of revenue gaps, compliance, and performance.
- **Voice Briefing:** AI-generated executive summaries for each strategic pillar.

#### 2. The Architect Studio (Manager Admin)
A high-productivity workspace for "Super Users" to build and modify the strategic architecture.
- **Pillar Construction:** Create and define focus areas, objectives, and themes.
- **Tactical Grid:** Direct entry for priorities with automated tracking of owners and deadlines.
- **Collaborator Management:** Centralized hub for inviting staff (Brad, Stephanie, Jessica, etc.) and managing organizational access.

#### 3. Agentic Strategic Operator (The "Second Brain")
A persistent AI assistant integrated into the UI.
- **Direct Execution:** Voice or text commands to add tasks, navigate views, or sync notebook data.
- **Second Brain Ingestion:** Stores institutional knowledge, meeting notes, and strategy nuances in a cloud-synced knowledge base.
- **Outbox Integration:** Agentic capability to draft/send strategic emails and schedule calendar events directly from strategic discussions.

### Target Audience
- **Athletic Director / Executive Staff:** For high-stakes organizational transformation.
- **Strategy & Operations Managers:** For maintaining the execution cadence.
- **SaaS Organizations:** Scalable framework for any entity requiring a "System-centric" rather than "Personality-centric" model.

---

*StratOS is built on a modern stack: React 19, Tailwind CSS, Google Gemini 2.5/3 series AI, and Supabase.*
